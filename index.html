Dots and Boxes

Peer-to-Peer Multiplayer

Game Setup

How to Play:

  • Create a game to get a unique game ID to share
  • Join a game by entering a game ID from another player
  • Players take turns connecting dots with horizontal or vertical lines
  • Complete a box to earn a point and get another turn
  • Blue lines are Player 1, red lines are Player 2
  • The player with the most boxes wins!
